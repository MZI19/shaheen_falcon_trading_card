Shaheen Falcon

The Shaheen Falcon can reach a dive speed of 320 kmh.

  • Scientfic Name: Falco Peregrinius Peregrinator
  • Height: 380 - 440 mm
  • Flight Speed: 240 kmh
  • Conservation: Vulnerable

The Shaheen Falcon is a predatory bird found in the Indian subcontinent. The complete black face mask is sharply demarcated from the white throat. The male Shaheen is smaller than the female Shaheen.
